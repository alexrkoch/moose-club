About the Club

The Moose Club is an organization committed to celebrating and honoring the glory of Moose. Moose is a 2-year-old German Shepherd, who was born in Bakersfield, California, and is currently residing in Apex, North Carolina. Miracles and good fortune touch all those around him.

In his free time Moose enjoys engaging with his community at the dog park or hiking off leash in the woods. Moose is a connoisseur of greenies and other dental chew treats, as well as various bones and squeaky toys. he is a world class ball chaser, and is currently working on his fetching skills.
